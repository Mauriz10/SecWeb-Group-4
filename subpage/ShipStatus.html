Shipping status.

It starts from receiving the product to the receiving point to the delivery point. Shipping status gives the in information of the objects current position. It provide an rough time period and condition of the product that it will take to reach to the destination. Control over shipping status is mainly in the hand of the different check points and the finally to the delivery person which is responsible to deliver the product to itâ€™s destination .

  1. 1. At every check points goods need to be checked about its condition and quickly recorded in the system.
  2. 2. Only authorized personal only should have access to the system. The wrong status may result a problem.
  3. 3. Final delivery person should also double check about the status and they should carefully deliver the product.
  4. 4. If the status of delivery needs to be updated immediately to the system.
  5. 5. If somehow the product is not delivered to the destination it his/her responsibility to take the product to the checkpoint and update the undelivered status to the system.
