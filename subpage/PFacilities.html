Partner Security Facility

The employee who needs to have access to our facility need to read our guideline “Security guide for property, plant and equipment”. He is only allowed to get access to areas of the which are necessary for his workspace. It is important that the employee walk only on the safety walkways, which are marked with yellow tape on the ground. Also, he needs to wear the right safety equipment. Otherwise the danger, that he can get injured during his stay in our facility is to big. He always needs to wear his company id, which shows that he is permitted to access our facility, visible. The employee is not allowed to touch or take something. People who are not reporting to our company, don´t have the rights to access our facility. If somebody get caught by trying to steal something, will have to expect legal consequences. If the employee is disregarding the guideline “Security guide for property, plant and equipment”, he can lose the permission to work with our software.
